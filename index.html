Dungeons and Dragons

Bear with me here - all interactive user design concepts lead to D and D. Just as all modern RPG video games one way or another, lead here. D and D is the foundation that launched our entire modern era of gaming and consequently user experience design. The entire premise of this nerd culture cornerstone is testing - then exceeding - the limits of what is possible when multiple people interact with the same concept. I am a newly minted Dungeon Master (meaning I run and design the entire UX for my players) and can attest to there being few things more intellectually challenging than designing a fun interactive experience for multiple players with diverse (sometimes opposing) goals for the game.
